Which country has the best food?

Food can be an essential part of travel. Indeed for some, it might be THE reason for travel. What countries have the best food though?

How do you even quantify it though? User ratings might be one, though it could have its biases.

Let's look at some countries' Zomato user ratings across restaurants. We can, from the limited data, see:

1: Which country has the best average ratings per restaurant that was rated

2: Which country has the cheapest prices per person on average

3: Which country has the best rated food for the cheapest (aka, where should a foodie on a budget go for a holiday?)

Hmm, looks like certain parts of the world might be better than others for foodies?
